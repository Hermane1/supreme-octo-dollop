Step by Step Directions to Make Princess Tiana's Gumbo

Preparation

Gathering Ingredients

  1. Start with a large pot and heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the flour and stir continuously to make a roux. Cook until it turns a dark brown color, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add chopped onions, bell peppers, and celery. Cook until they are tender.
  4. Stir in the garlic, thyme, and bay leaves.
  5. Add sliced sausage and cook until browned.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Add the chicken, okra, and tomatoes.
  8. Reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes.
  9. Season with salt, pepper, and hot sauce to taste.
  10. Serve hot over rice.
